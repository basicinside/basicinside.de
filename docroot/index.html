about me

(today) developer and cofounder of 42reports.com
CoFounder. Development of the WiFi sensor firmware (OpenWRT, C, cURL, TPACKET_V3). Development of scalable sensor data collection and storage (Tornado, Pika, Rabbitmq, Postgresql, AWS). Development of rapid firmware installation system (Flask, Paramiko, cURL, VLAN). Various features from frontend (React, Angular) to backend (Django, Postgresql).
(today) study compsci at Berlin Institute of Technology
(2009-2013) student worker at Berlin Open Wireless Lab
Development of Web-Control and Management Client-Server solution for OpenWRT based Routers, Indoor and Outdoor Testbed Maintenance and Extension (>100 routers), Maintenance and Development of OpenWRT based Distribution, System Administration (mainly XEN, KVM virtual machines, 802.1x, DHCP, Nagios), Collaboration with researchers and Bsc./Msc./PhD students.
(*-2013) freifunker
Development of a monitoring, ranking system and map for the freifunk community routers (Ruby on Rails, OpenLayers). Maintainer of Piratenfreifunk OpenWRT packages (Lua, OpenWRT, Shell).
